About Culturei Deliveries

Delivering More Than Orders Across South Africa.

Who We Are

Welcome to Culturei Deliveries, a proudly South African delivery platform owned and operated by CULTURE (PTY) LTD.

We connect customers with their favourite restaurants through a reliable network of independent delivery drivers, making food delivery simple, fast and convenient.

Our Vision

To become one of South Africa's most trusted delivery platforms by building strong partnerships with restaurants, empowering drivers and providing customers with an exceptional delivery experience.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering outstanding customer service while helping local restaurants grow and creating earning opportunities for independent drivers.

How Culturei Deliveries Works

  1. Customers browse restaurants and place their orders through the Culturei Deliveries platform.
  2. Restaurants receive and prepare the order.
  3. An independent delivery driver collects the order.
  4. The order is delivered safely to the customer.
  5. Restaurants and drivers receive scheduled weekly payouts according to their agreements with Culturei Deliveries.

Why Choose Culturei Deliveries?

  • Fast and reliable delivery.
  • Secure payment processing.
  • Professional customer support.
  • Trusted partnerships with local restaurants.
  • Opportunities for independent drivers.

Supporting Local Businesses

We believe local businesses are the heart of our communities. Our goal is to help restaurants reach more customers while providing an affordable, reliable and professional delivery service.

As Culturei Deliveries grows, we remain committed to honesty, transparency and continuous improvement in everything we do.
