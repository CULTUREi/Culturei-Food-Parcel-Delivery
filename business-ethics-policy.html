Business Ethics Policy

At Culturei Deliveries, integrity is at the heart of everything we do. This Business Ethics Policy outlines the principles that guide our employees, directors, restaurant partners, independent drivers, contractors and stakeholders in conducting business honestly, professionally and responsibly.

1. Our Ethical Commitment

Culturei Deliveries is committed to conducting business with honesty, fairness, integrity and accountability. Ethical conduct protects our reputation and strengthens the confidence of customers, restaurant partners, independent drivers, regulators, investors and the communities we serve.

We are committed to:

  • Acting honestly.
  • Treating people fairly.
  • Respecting the law.
  • Protecting confidential information.
  • Making responsible business decisions.
  • Building relationships based on trust.
  • Conducting business with professionalism and integrity.

Every decision we make should reflect our values and strengthen confidence in the Culturei Deliveries brand.

2. Integrity in Business

Employees, contractors and representatives of Culturei Deliveries are expected to uphold the highest standards of integrity in every business interaction.

  • Tell the truth.
  • Honour commitments.
  • Avoid misleading statements.
  • Conduct business fairly.
  • Accept responsibility for their actions.
  • Correct mistakes openly and promptly.

3. Respect for People

Culturei Deliveries is committed to treating every individual with dignity, fairness and respect. We believe respectful relationships create stronger business partnerships and better customer experiences.

This commitment extends to:

  • Customers.
  • Employees.
  • Restaurant partners.
  • Independent delivery drivers.
  • Suppliers.
  • Government authorities.
  • Members of the public.

We value diversity and encourage an inclusive environment where everyone is treated professionally and respectfully.

4. Compliance with Laws

Culturei Deliveries conducts its business in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa and all applicable regulations.

We are committed to complying with:

  • South African legislation.
  • Applicable regulations.
  • Industry standards.
  • Contractual obligations.
  • Internal company policies.

Compliance represents the minimum standard. Wherever possible, Culturei Deliveries strives to exceed legal requirements through ethical leadership and responsible business practices.

5. Fair Competition

Culturei Deliveries supports fair, transparent and lawful competition. We compete by providing outstanding service, innovation and professionalism.

The company will never engage in:

  • Unfair business practices.
  • False or misleading advertising.
  • Anti-competitive conduct.
  • Unethical interference with competitors.
  • Misrepresentation of products or services.

Every employee and representative is expected to uphold these principles when dealing with customers, suppliers and competitors.

6. Conflicts of Interest

Business decisions must always be made in the best interests of Culturei Deliveries.

Employees, contractors and representatives should avoid situations where personal interests may influence, or appear to influence, business decisions.

Potential conflicts should be disclosed promptly to management.

Examples include:

  • Financial interests that conflict with company interests.
  • Relationships that may influence decision-making.
  • Outside employment creating divided loyalties.
  • Personal gain from company opportunities.

7. Confidentiality & Privacy

Culturei Deliveries respects confidential business information and the privacy of customers, restaurant partners, drivers and employees.

Confidential information obtained through business activities must only be used for legitimate business purposes and protected against unauthorized access or disclosure.

The company is committed to safeguarding personal information in accordance with applicable privacy legislation, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) where applicable.

8. Responsible Use of Company Resources

Company assets and resources should be used responsibly, efficiently and only for authorised business purposes.

Resources include:

  • Financial assets.
  • Technology systems.
  • Equipment.
  • Vehicles.
  • Intellectual property.
  • Company information.
  • Employee time and expertise.

Employees and representatives are expected to avoid waste, misuse, unauthorised personal use or damage to company resources.

9. Gifts, Hospitality and Business Courtesies

Business gifts, hospitality and courtesies should never influence, or appear to influence, business decisions.

Employees and representatives should never:

  • Offer inappropriate gifts.
  • Accept excessive gifts.
  • Request personal favours.
  • Exchange benefits for preferential treatment.

Any gift or hospitality that could create a conflict of interest should be declared in accordance with company procedures.

10. Social Responsibility

Culturei Deliveries recognises its responsibility to contribute positively to society and the communities in which we operate.

We are committed to:

  • Supporting local businesses.
  • Promoting responsible business practices.
  • Encouraging ethical partnerships.
  • Respecting local communities.
  • Minimising unnecessary environmental impact where reasonably practicable.

We believe our success should contribute to the wellbeing and economic growth of the communities we serve.

11. Reporting Ethical Concerns

Anyone who becomes aware of unethical conduct is encouraged to report the matter promptly.

Reports may include:

  • Dishonesty.
  • Fraud.
  • Corruption.
  • Harassment.
  • Discrimination.
  • Safety concerns.
  • Conflicts of interest.
  • Serious policy violations.

Reports made honestly and in good faith will be treated respectfully, investigated appropriately and handled confidentially where possible.

12. Accountability

Every employee, manager and leader is personally responsible for upholding the ethical standards of Culturei Deliveries.

Managers are expected to lead by example and foster a workplace culture built on integrity, honesty and accountability.

13. Continuous Improvement

Culturei Deliveries is committed to regularly reviewing and improving its ethical practices, governance framework and internal policies.

We encourage continuous learning, open dialogue and ongoing improvement so our ethical standards continue to evolve as the business grows.

14. Executive Commitment

The leadership of Culturei Deliveries recognises that reputation is earned through consistent ethical behaviour.

We believe trust cannot be purchased—it must be earned through honesty, fairness, transparency and accountability.

Every decision we make should strengthen the confidence that customers, restaurant partners, independent delivery drivers, employees and stakeholders place in Culturei Deliveries.

By conducting business ethically today, we build an organisation that future generations can respect tomorrow.
