1. Purpose

This Restaurant Partner Policy sets out the standards, responsibilities and operational requirements for all restaurants partnering with Culturei Deliveries.

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that customers receive high-quality food, reliable service and a consistent delivery experience while supporting restaurant partners in growing their businesses.

All restaurant partners are expected to comply with this policy, the Restaurant Partner Agreement and all applicable South African laws and regulations.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To become a Restaurant Partner, a business must:

  • Operate as a legitimate food business.
  • Hold all licences, permits and registrations required by law.
  • Comply with applicable food safety and health regulations.
  • Provide accurate business information during registration.
  • Supply valid banking details for payouts.
  • Agree to the Culturei Deliveries Restaurant Partner Agreement.
  • Meet the operational standards required by Culturei Deliveries.

Culturei Deliveries reserves the right to approve or decline any partnership application at its discretion.

3. Restaurant Onboarding

Before activation on the platform, restaurant partners may be required to provide:

  • Business registration details (where applicable).
  • Owner or authorised representative identification.
  • Contact information.
  • Banking details.
  • Trading address.
  • Trading hours.
  • Menu with accurate descriptions and pricing.
  • Company logo and promotional images (where available).

Restaurants are responsible for ensuring that all information supplied remains accurate and up to date.

4. Order Management

Restaurant partners are expected to:

  • Monitor incoming orders during operating hours.
  • Accept or decline orders promptly.
  • Begin food preparation immediately after accepting an order.
  • Notify Culturei Deliveries of any delays or stock shortages.
  • Mark orders as "Ready for Collection" only when fully prepared.

Timely order management helps minimise waiting times for customers and delivery drivers.

5. Food Quality

Restaurant partners remain solely responsible for:

  • Food preparation.
  • Food quality.
  • Taste and presentation.
  • Portion sizes.
  • Food freshness.
  • Compliance with food safety standards.

Culturei Deliveries does not prepare, cook or alter food products and therefore cannot guarantee the quality of food prepared by restaurant partners.

6. Packaging Standards

Restaurants must package orders appropriately to ensure safe transportation.

Packaging should:

  • Prevent spills and leaks.
  • Protect food quality during delivery.
  • Keep hot food hot and cold food cold where reasonably possible.
  • Clearly identify customer orders.
  • Include all ordered items.

Poor packaging may lead to customer complaints and negatively affect service quality.

7. Collection by Delivery Drivers

When a driver arrives, restaurant staff should:

  • Verify the order number.
  • Confirm the customer's name.
  • Hand over the correct order.
  • Ensure all items are included.
  • Confirm that packaging is properly sealed where applicable.

Restaurants should avoid unnecessary delays once an order is marked as ready.

8. Menu Accuracy

Restaurants are responsible for maintaining accurate menu information.

Menu listings should include:

  • Correct prices.
  • Accurate descriptions.
  • Available options and extras.
  • Allergy information where applicable.
  • Availability of menu items.

Menu updates should be made promptly to avoid customer disappointment.

9. Customer Service

Restaurant partners are expected to:

  • Treat customers and delivery drivers professionally.
  • Cooperate with Culturei Deliveries Customer Support.
  • Respond to operational enquiries promptly.
  • Assist in resolving customer concerns where required.

Positive customer experiences strengthen both the restaurant's reputation and the Culturei Deliveries brand.

10. Payments and Weekly Payouts

Restaurant earnings are calculated based on completed customer orders, less any agreed service fees, commissions or other authorised deductions.

Payments are made according to the scheduled weekly payout cycle.

Restaurants are responsible for ensuring that their banking information remains accurate.

11. Cancellations and Refunds

Restaurants should avoid cancelling accepted orders unless absolutely necessary.

Where cancellation becomes unavoidable, the restaurant should notify Culturei Deliveries immediately.

Refunds and payment adjustments will be handled in accordance with the Culturei Deliveries Refund and Cancellation Policy.

12. Food Safety and Legal Compliance

Restaurant partners are responsible for complying with all applicable South African laws relating to:

  • Food hygiene.
  • Health and safety.
  • Consumer protection.
  • Business licensing.
  • Labour laws applicable to their own employees.
  • Environmental health requirements.

Culturei Deliveries may request evidence of compliance where appropriate.

13. Performance Standards

Culturei Deliveries monitors service quality to maintain a high standard across the platform.

Performance may be assessed using factors such as:

  • Order acceptance rate.
  • Preparation times.
  • Order accuracy.
  • Customer feedback.
  • Complaint history.
  • Reliability.
  • Compliance with company policies.

Where performance issues arise, Culturei Deliveries may work with the restaurant to improve service before taking further action.

14. Suspension or Removal from the Platform

Culturei Deliveries reserves the right to temporarily suspend or permanently remove a restaurant partner from the platform where necessary.

Reasons may include:

  • Serious food safety concerns.
  • Fraudulent activity.
  • Repeated failure to meet operational standards.
  • Misrepresentation of products or pricing.
  • Persistent customer complaints.
  • Breach of the Restaurant Partner Agreement.
  • Failure to comply with applicable laws.

Where reasonably possible, the restaurant will be informed of the reasons and given an opportunity to respond before permanent removal.

15. Partnership Commitment

Culturei Deliveries values long-term relationships with restaurant partners.

We are committed to:

  • Supporting business growth.
  • Providing reliable technology.
  • Offering professional customer support.
  • Maintaining transparent communication.
  • Continuously improving our platform and services.

Together with our restaurant partners, we aim to provide customers across South Africa with a fast, reliable and enjoyable food delivery experience.
