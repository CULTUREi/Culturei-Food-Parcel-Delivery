About Culturei

Our Story

Culturei was born from a simple idea: Food and connection are the universal languages of culture.

We connect South Africans with authentic, home-style meals from local chefs who pour their heritage into every dish. Whether you're craving a spicy Cape Malay curry, a traditional braai, or a quick parcel delivery across town, Culturei brings it to your door.

We're proud to support local restaurants and provide meaningful work for drivers across Mzansi. Every order creates opportunity and celebrates the rich diversity of South African culture.

Our Mission

To connect people through food and delivery, celebrating South Africa's diverse cultures.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted delivery platform in Africa, empowering local businesses and drivers.

Our Values

Integrity, community, innovation, and celebrating the rich tapestry of South African culture.
